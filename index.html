02 / BIOGRAPHY

THE ARTIST

A convergence of traditional craftsmanship and progressive abstraction.
* KHADIJA SHAFAIE * FINE ARTIST

"Art is not a reflection of reality, but a dialogue with the unseen structures that form it."

Khadija Shafaie is a contemporary fine artist whose work explores the boundaries of medium density, structural geometry, and ambient tonal contrasts. Based in her private studio, she constructs multilayered panels that combine classic oil techniques with industrial minerals, fluid ink processes, and modern digital reflections.

Deeply inspired by Brutalist architecture, organic decay, and stellar topography, Khadija focuses on building intense tactile experiences. Her paint applications range from heavy impasto to fluid, transparent glazes, creating depth that shifts dynamically with the ambient light of the viewing space.
